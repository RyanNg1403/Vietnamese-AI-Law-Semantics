Artificial intelligence is the electronic implementation of human intellectual capabilities. Artificial intelligence system is a machine-based system designed to perform artificial intelligence capabilities with varying levels of autonomy. Developer is an organization or individual that designs, builds, trains, tests, or fine-tunes an artificial intelligence system. Provider is an organization or individual that brings an artificial intelligence system to the market. Deployer is an organization or individual that uses an artificial intelligence system for professional purposes. User is an organization or individual that directly interacts with an artificial intelligence system. Serious incident is an event occurring during the operation of an artificial intelligence system that causes significant damage. The damage may affect human life, health, property, or the environment.
